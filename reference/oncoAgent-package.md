# oncoAgent: Agentic AI-Driven Oncology Target Discovery from Multi-Omics Data

A semi-autonomous research system that integrates multi-omics data
(genomics, transcriptomics, CRISPR screens, drug sensitivity) with
literature mining and clinical outcomes to identify and prioritize
oncology drug targets. Features adaptive agentic workflows with gap
detection, hypothesis generation, and benchmarking against known cancer
gene sets (COSMIC, Open Targets). Designed for industry-grade target
discovery pipelines at pharma scale.
